### <T><span slot="en">How to Make It</span><span slot="ja">作り方</span><span slot="en_simple">How to Make It</span></T>

<T>
  <span slot="en">
    To put it simply, just add everything to the pot and cook it for 20-30
    minutes. It really is my kind of low-effort recipe. However, I think it
    will taste a lot better if you properly follow this order to build the
    flavor:
  </span>
  <span slot="ja">
    ざっくり言うと、全部を鍋に入れて20〜30分煮るだけです。本当に僕好みの手間が少ないレシピです。でも、味をしっかり作るために、この順番で進めたほうがかなりおいしくなると思います。
  </span>
  <span slot="en_simple">
    Making this is very easy. You can put everything in a pot. Cook for 20 to
    30 minutes. Please follow these steps:
  </span>
</T>

#### <T><span slot="en">1. Brown the meat</span><span slot="ja">1. 肉を焼く</span><span slot="en_simple">1. Cook the meat</span></T>

<T>
  <span slot="en">
    Heat the oil and cook the ground beef/pork mixture. Drain off any excess
    oil. Make sure to break the meat apart if it gets stuck in big chunks.
  </span>
  <span slot="ja">
    油を熱して、合いびき肉を炒めます。余分な油は取り除いてください。肉が大きなかたまりになったら、しっかりほぐしながら炒めるのがポイントです。
  </span>
  <span slot="en_simple">
    Heat the oil. Cook the meat. Take out the extra oil. Cut the meat into
    small pieces.
  </span>
</T>

#### <T><span slot="en">2. Saute the onions</span><span slot="ja">2. 玉ねぎを炒める</span><span slot="en_simple">2. Cook the onions</span></T>

<T>
  <span slot="en">
    Add the diced onions and let them cook for at least 5 minutes until
    softened.
  </span>
  <span slot="ja">
    角切りにした玉ねぎを加えて、やわらかくなるまで最低5分は炒めます。
  </span>
  <span slot="en_simple">
    Add the onions. Cook for 5 minutes.
  </span>
</T>

#### <T><span slot="en">3. Add spices</span><span slot="ja">3. スパイスを入れる</span><span slot="en_simple">3. Add spices</span></T>

<T>
  <span slot="en">
    Add the minced garlic, chili powder, cumin, and paprika. Stir and cook for
    about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  </span>
  <span slot="ja">
    みじん切りにしたにんにく、チリパウダー、クミン、パプリカを加えます。香りが立つまで、混ぜながら30秒ほど炒めます。
  </span>
  <span slot="en_simple">
    Add the garlic and spices. Mix well. Cook for 30 seconds.
  </span>
</T>

#### <T><span slot="en">4. Simmer</span><span slot="ja">4. 煮込む</span><span slot="en_simple">4. Cook together</span></T>

<T>
  <span slot="en">
    Finally, add the tomato paste, canned tomatoes, broth, and beans. Let the
    chili <Explain meaning="cook gently on low heat">simmer</Explain> on low
    heat for 20-30 minutes.
  </span>
  <span slot="ja">
    最後に、トマトペースト、トマト缶、スープ（または水）、豆を加えます。弱火で20〜30分煮込みます。
  </span>
  <span slot="en_simple">
    Add the tomato paste, tomatoes, soup, and beans. Cook on low heat for 20 to
    30 minutes.
  </span>
</T>

#### <T><span slot="en">5. Season</span><span slot="ja">5. 味を整える</span><span slot="en_simple">5. Season</span></T>

<T>
  <span slot="en">
    Add salt and black pepper{" "}
    <Explain meaning="as much as you like after tasting">to taste</Explain>{" "}
    right before serving.
  </span>
  <span slot="ja">
    食べる直前に、塩と黒こしょうで味を整えます。
  </span>
  <span slot="en_simple">
    Add salt and pepper.
  </span>
</T>
