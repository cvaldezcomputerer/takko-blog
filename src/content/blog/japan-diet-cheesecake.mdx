### <T><span slot="en">The Mistake</span><span slot="ja">大失敗</span><span slot="en_simple">No!</span></T>

<T>
  <span slot="en">Actually, the first time I tried to make this, I <Explain meaning="did not do something">skipped</Explain> putting it in the fridge. You need to let it sit in the fridge because the dry crackers <Explain meaning="soak up">absorb</Explain> the liquid from the Greek yogurt.   <span slot="ja">実は、初めて作った時は冷蔵庫に入れずに食べてしまいました。これは大失敗でした。味が全然違います。乾燥したサブレがヨーグルトの水分を吸う必要があるので、冷蔵庫で寝かせないといけません。そうすることで、ヨーグルトは固くなり、サブレは柔らかくなります。食感が本物のチーズケーキに近くなるんです。</span>
  <span slot="en_simple">The first time, I did not use the fridge. That was a mistake. In the fridge, the cookies take in water from the yogurt. Then, the yogurt becomes hard and the cookies become soft. It feels like real cheesecake.</span>
